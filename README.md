# 🧵 KIDS-CLASH
**Turning Play into Progress.** EduTug is a zero-latency, web-based multiplayer educational game that transforms mental math and vocabulary into a competitive, digital "Tug-of-War" team sport. Built specifically for classroom Smart Boards and touch screens, it shifts EdTech from "single-player passive learning" to "multi-player active learning."

## ✨ Core Features

* **🎮 Continuous Flow State Gameplay:** As soon as a team answers, a new question instantly appears. No waiting, no lag.
* **🧮 Smart Math Engine:** Dynamically generates random equations for Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, and Mixed Operations.
* **📚 Ultimate English Engine:** Tests multiple vocabulary skills including Synonyms, Antonyms, Missing Letters, Word Pairs, Vowels, and Plurals.
* **🚫 The "Penalty Lock" System:** If a student guesses incorrectly, their side flashes red, shakes, and locks their buttons for exactly 1 second to prevent answer-spamming.
* **📊 Teacher Analytics Dashboard:** While students play, the engine silently tracks data. At the end of the 60-second round, it generates a report showing Correct Answers, Mistakes, and Accuracy Percentages for both teams.
* **⚡ Zero-Latency Architecture:** Built entirely on Vanilla Web Technologies. No heavy frameworks, no installation required. It runs instantly in any modern web browser.

## 🚀 How to Play

1. **Select a Subject:** Choose between Mathematics or English.
2. **Choose a Mode:** Select your difficulty or operation type.
3. **The Battle:** The 60-second timer begins! Two teams stand at the Smart Board and tap the correct answers.
4. **Pull the Rope:** Every correct answer physically pulls the rope and the characters toward your side.
5. **Win:** The team with the most points when the clock hits zero wins the round!
